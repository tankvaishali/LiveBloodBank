UNVEILLING NEW LOOK

The Social Army group's important project, connected with the heart, is called the "Live Blood Bank". In this project, as you all know, during emergencies or when there is a need for specific blood groups, it becomes challenging to find suitable donors. To address this challenge, the Live Blood Bank project connects a vast network of blood donors across various cities and states through a centralized system. These donors selflessly offer blood to serve others.
Now, the Live Blood Bank is being relaunched with a fresh look and interface, where you all can witness a new web platform. If you wish to contribute blood altruistically, you can register on the website and receive information. Additionally, a mobile application for the Live Blood Bank is also in the pipeline, which will make it easier for you to arrange blood donations during emergencies. You will also be able to donate blood conveniently through this application.

DONATION PROCESS

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

Registration

You need to complete a very simple registration form. Which contains all required contact information to enter in the donation process.

Screening

A drop of blood from your finger will take for simple test to ensure that your blood iron levels are proper enough for donation process.

Donation

After ensuring and passed screening test successfully you will be directed to a donor bed for donation. It will take only 6-10 minutes.

Refreshment

You can also stay in sitting room until you feel strong enough to leave our center. You will receive awesome drink from us in donation zone.
